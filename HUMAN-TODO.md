# Human-Only Items

These items need a person, device, account, clinician, or policy decision.

- Clinical sign-off: have a qualified physiotherapist or tutor approve exercises, dosage, red flags, and pain education before use with a real patient.
- Real-device install checks: test iPhone Safari install, Android Chrome install, offline reopen, and native share behavior.
- Print validation: print the magnets on the actual material and scan all five QR codes with at least one iPhone and one Android phone.
- Patient trial: get consent, trial with a real patient, record feedback privately, and keep identifying details out of this public repository.
- Git history privacy decision: the published history contains older personal author metadata and patient-case commit subjects. Rewriting public history or replacing the repository is a human owner decision.
